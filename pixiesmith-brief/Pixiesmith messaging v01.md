## Overview

* Narrative is the foundation—it sets the emotional story and broader purpose.  
* Messaging is the execution—it ensures the brand communicates that story consistently across different channels.  
* Values are the north stars—they shaspe how the brand acts, makes decisions, and interacts with its audience, employees, and partners.

## **Purpose (internal)** {#purpose-(internal)}

*Why do you exist beyond making money?*

* To accelerate a new business model where positive change matters more than indefinite scale.   
* To champion a new paradigm of business, one where the measure of success is positive change rather than indefinite growth.   
* To create consciousness around the benefits around materials that are designed to die.

## **Philosophy** {#philosophy}

*Some brands do have a philosophy statement, but it’s not always a standard component of branding. When used, it typically serves as a deeply rooted belief system that guides decision-making, culture, and messaging. It’s less about marketing and more about the brand’s ethos and long-term vision.*

Designed to die: Every one of our pieces exists with a purpose, a cycle, and a deliberate end. We will create 78 collections, each aligned with a tarot card, and when the deck is complete, Pixiesmith will dissolve, reinvent, or reinvest into something meaningful. Our business model is built to challenge an unsustainable economic system based on infinite growth and illustrate how things can be done differently.

# **Guidelines** {#guidelines}

1) Tarot is our framework, not our identity.  
* We frame tarot as a visual language of transformation, rather than a belief system.  
* We focus on the universal themes, symbolism, and archetypal storytelling that tarot provides rather than framing it as purely spiritual.  
* We focus on storytelling, personal transformation, and bold individuality rather than esoteric mysticism.  
* We lean into artistic and conceptual inspirations from tarot rather than positioning it as a fixed, spiritual guide.  
* We use strong, empowering messaging that appeals to those who love fashion as a statement of identity and ambition.

2) Sustainability is the stakes, not the selling point.  
* We are philosophy-led not sustainability-led.   
* Our vision questions the entire foundation of what it means to be a sustainable business and consumer, rather than simply offering eco-friendly materials as a checkbox.  
* Sustainability is woven into our DNA but does not define our brand.  
* We acknowledge the complexities and contradictions of sustainability in fashion—we do our best within an imperfect system.  
* Our ethos is about circularity, intentionality, and impermanence—challenging the obsession with endless growth.  
* We believe in designing with a clear end in mind—from our garments to our company.  
* Our consumers are buying into a philosophy of conscious transformation, not just a sustainable product.

3) Magic and craft is merged seamlessly—it is felt rather than stated  
* Our name—Pixie (magic) \+ Smith (skill)—embodies this fusion, guiding our communication style.

  *Craft*

* Craft is our grounding force—the tactile, intentional counterpart to our ethereal magic.  
* We honor the artistry behind every collection, from the sourcing of thoughtful materials to the final stitch.  
*  Every fiber, finish, and form is chosen with care. We create consciously, with deep respect for the hands that make our garments, the heritage of craft that informs them, and the ecosystems we touch.  
* In a world driven by speed and disposability, we choose to slow down—to elevate materials, refine technique, and allow quality to speak.  
* This devotion to craftsmanship is how we earn trust. It’s how our values become tangible. It’s what anchors our magic—and ensures it endures.


	*Magic* 

* While we don’t lead with magic in our tagline or name, it remains a powerful underlying theme that clarifies our vision.  
* Rather than saying we create magic, we let our audience experience it—through clothing, digital storytelling, and immersive activations.  
* This approach ensures our magic feels aspirational and individual, rather than Disney-esque fantasy or preachy idealism.  
* For us, magic is a catalyst following a period of internal transformation—the moment when introspection turns into action, and the impossible becomes possible.  
* It’s woven into our creative process, our collections, and the journey of our consumers.  
* Our audience shares this ethos. We attract visionaries, creators, and self-reinventors—people who believe in possibility, personal evolution, and shaping their own reality.  
* Whether or not they seek to change the world, they are bold enough to change themselves.

# **Tone of voice** {#tone-of-voice}

*Key words:* Confident, intriguing, slightly cryptic, magnetic, aspirational and enigmatic

Pixiesmith’s communication should be an invitation to believe in something greater. We aren’t just selling fashion; we are building a visionary narrative that encourages self-transformation, bold individuality, and a reimagining of the future. Every word should feel like an aspirational prophecy, guiding our audience toward a new way of thinking, dressing, and existing.

### 

1) Speak to the future not just the present: Our messaging paints a bigger picture of transformation, possibility, and evolution. Pixiesmith is not about trends; it’s about what’s next and who you’re becoming.

2) Inspiration over information: Our words should make people feel like they are part of a movement, not just consumers of a brand. Every message should be an invitation to self-expression and change.

3) Impermanence as an underlying truth: Like a prophecy, our messaging conveys inevitable transformation. What we create isn’t meant to last forever—it’s meant to serve a purpose, evolve, and then shift into something new. 

4) Evocative and surreal: Our language should feel conceptually bold, artistically weird, and emotionally resonant. It’s not about shock value—it’s about drawing people in with unexpected ideas and intriguing contradictions.

5) Confident, visionary, and self-assured: We speak with vision and conviction, never doubting our purpose. Our tone makes people feel powerful, capable, and excited to step into the unknown.

6) Community-driven: Prophecies are meant to be shared and believed in collectively. Pixiesmith messaging should always foster a sense of belonging, exclusivity, and deeper meaning—as if those who understand the brand are part of something special, rare, and transformative.

7) Duality of playfulness and depth: Our messaging is playful and laced with hidden layers. Like an inside joke or a secret message, engaging with Pixiesmith feels exclusive, immersive, and slightly enigmatic.

# **Values** {#values}

Your values guide your brand behaviors and decisions. While these do not need to be expressed externally, they can serve as a framework to help shape your culture, messaging, and customer interactions—ultimately leading to a stronger brand identity and reputation.

## **Impermanence**  {#impermanence}

Pixiesmith embraces impermanence—we are a brand designed to die, not endlessly scale. Our finite collections, inspired by tarot’s 78-card cycle, challenge the idea that brands must last forever. Like tarot, our philosophy embraces cycles: creation, transformation, and renewal. This extends to our materials, mindset, and business model, ensuring we leave a positive impact when we end. 

## **Irreverence** {#irreverence}

We are bold, magnetic, and effortlessly rebellious—subtly challenging conventions with surrealism and wit. Our playfully defiant approach disrupts traditional business models, fashion norms, and industry expectations—without being preachy or predictable.

## **Quality**  {#quality}

At Pixiesmith, quality is a commitment. Our designs are crafted to be felt as much as they are seen and our clothes feel luxurious. We use organic, compostable materials and artisanal craftsmanship to create standout pieces. We want you to feel good wearing our clothes—inside and out.

## **Storytelling** {#storytelling}

Every piece we create is a story in motion, waiting to be worn. Our collections empower customers to see, shape, and step into their evolving identities, weaving fashion into a larger movement of self-reflection, creativity, and transformation.

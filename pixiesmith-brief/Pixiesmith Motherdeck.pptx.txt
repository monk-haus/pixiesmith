A company designed to die.
Pixiesmith has pre-planned every one of our “drops,” and we will cease to exist once they are complete. Then, we will become something entirely new: a phoenix company.

Our aim is to introduce a new business philosophy. We believe that the incentive to endlessly grow and scale companies is leading our planet into a state of polycrisis. So, what if we designed our death in a way that has a positive impact?

What is a phoenix company?
Phoenix companies rise from emerging Stanford Business School theory that reimagines impact entrepreneurship. The ideology continues to evolve over time but, in its essence, it invites businesses to rise and fall and reinvent themselves rather than aim to accumulate assets forever — thus enabling them to create positive impact in new ways. 


@pixiesmithofficial
We are inspired by Patagonia, a fashion company that has made planet Earth its only shareholder, fundamentally proposing a new purpose for the role of business.

About Us
Page 01
Pixiesmith is a phoenix company that exists to challenge the conventional norms of the fashion industry. Unlike traditional companies driven by endless growth and accumulation, we are committed to a singular vision—bringing the mystical beauty of the Rider-Waite Tarot deck to life through exquisite clothing. Using only 100% natural materials and collaborating with like-minded partners, we craft each collection with intention and reverence for the Earth. Our journey will conclude once our mission is fulfilled, embodying the essence of renewal and regeneration.
Our Mission
Our Vision
Our mission is to inspire transformation through fashion while championing conservation and sustainability. We are dedicated to creating a unique capsule wardrobe for each card in the Rider-Waite Tarot deck, blending art and symbolism to evoke personal and collective growth. Our journey is finite and purposeful—once the entire deck is complete, we will gracefully dissolve, leaving behind a legacy that supports ecological preservation and reflects our commitment to a more balanced future. 
We envision a world where fashion serves as a catalyst for change, encouraging introspection and fostering a deep connection with the environment. Through our tarot-inspired collections, we aim to cultivate a community of mindful consumers who value creativity, quality, and sustainability. By investing all profits in conservation projects, we aspire to leave a positive, lasting impact on our planet and its inhabitants, creating a timeless legacy through our work.
